Pager

You can create a pager by specifying the uc-pager class.

1. Basic

Create a basic pager.

  • 1
  • 2
  • 3
  • 4
  • ...
  • 10
<div class="uc-pager-unit">
  <ul class="uc-pager">
    <li class="_current"><span>1</span></li>
    <li><a href="#"><span>2</span></a></li>
    <li><a href="#"><span>3</span></a></li>
    <li><a href="#"><span>4</span></a></li>
    <li class="_ghost"><span>...</span></li>
    <li><a href="#"><span>10</span></a></li>
  </ul>
</div>

2. Centering

By specifying the _center class, you can center the elements.

  • 2

<div class="uc-pager-unit _center">

    <li><a href="#"><span>3</span></a></li>

  </ul>
</div>
